CHARGE
Main Action: Move up to speed in a straight line, then make a melee free strike. Cannot move through difficult terrain or shift.
DEFEND
Main Action: Ability rolls against you take double bane. You gain double edge on environmental resistance tests. No benefit while taunted.
AID ATTACK
Maneuver: Choose adjacent enemy. Next ally's ability roll against them gains edge until start of your next turn.

üèÉ Movement Actions

SHIFTING
Careful movement that prevents opportunity attacks. Cannot shift into or through difficult/damaging terrain. Can choose to move normally instead.
DISENGAGE
Move Action: Shift 1 square. Can break up movement with maneuver and main action. Only once per round.
RIDE
Move Action: While mounted, move your mount up to its speed OR have mount Disengage. Once per round per mount.
BURROW & DIG
Burrow: Move through dirt horizontally at full speed (requires burrow speed).
Dig: Maneuver to move vertically up to your size in squares (requires burrow speed ‚â• size).
Claw Dirt
Maneuver ‚Ä¢ Self
Power Roll + Might:
11-: Move 1 square into, out of, or through burrowable ground you're touching and become slowed and weakened (EoT).
12-16: You can use your main action this turn to move 1 square into, out of, or through burrowable ground you're touching and become slowed (EoT).
17+: Move up to 1 square into, out of, or through burrowable ground you're touching.
Burrowing Forced Movement
A burrowing creature that is completely beneath the ground ignores non-vertical forced movement and instead takes 1 damage per square it would have moved. Vertical forced movement moves the creature through the dirt as normal.
CLIMB & SWIM
With special movement: full speed. Without: 2 movement per square. Difficult surfaces require Might test (failure wastes no movement).
CLIMB OTHER CREATURES
You can attempt to climb a creature whose size is greater than yours. Climbing a willing creature requires no test.
Climb Unwilling Creature
Power Roll + Might or Agility:
11-: You fail to climb the creature and it can make a free strike against you.
12-16: You fail to climb the creature.
17+: You climb the creature.

Hold On
Power Roll + Might or Agility:
11-: You fall off into an unoccupied adjacent space of your choice, taking falling damage and landing prone as normal.
12-16: You slide down into an unoccupied adjacent space of your choice and don't land prone.
17+: You continue to hold on to the creature.
If you are knocked prone while climbing or riding a creature, you fall into an adjacent space of your choice, taking the usual falling damage and landing prone.
JUMP
Base: Might or Agility score squares long, 1 square high.
Enhanced: Test for extra distance:
11-: No bonus
12-16: +1 square
17+: +2 squares

ü•∑ Stealth Actions

HIDE & SNEAK
Hide: Requires cover/concealment from unobserving enemies. Maneuver in combat, automatic if conditions met.
Benefits: Cannot be targeted by non-Area abilities. Edge on ability rolls until end of turn after becoming unhidden.
Sneak: While hidden outside combat, move at half speed with Agility test to stay hidden.

üîß Utility Actions

ASSIST A TEST
Make a test to help another creature:
11-: Bane on their test
12-16: Edge on their test
17+: Double edge on their test
CATCH BREATH
Maneuver: Spend a Recovery to regain Stamina equal to your recovery value. Cannot use while dying.
USE CONSUMABLE
Maneuver: Activate a consumable treasure (potion, etc.) on yourself or adjacent willing creature.
STAND UP
Maneuver: End prone condition on yourself or adjacent willing creature.
MAKE OR ASSIST A TEST
Most tests are maneuvers in combat (search, pick lock, lift). Complex tests may require actions. Instant tests are free maneuvers.
