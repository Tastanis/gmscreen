TALENT

A rare few people are born with the
            potential to harness psionic power,
            but only those who experience an
awakening, a significant event that activates
a talent's abilities, can tap into the mind's
full potential. You are one of those people--a
master of psionics and a source of incredible
power created through sheer force of will.
You can move and change matter, time,
gravity, the laws of physics, or another
creature's mind.

As a talent, you are limited only by the
strength of your mind. But the ability to
wield multiple powers at once and change
reality at will involves a gamble. Every
manifestation has a chance of harming you,
and talents who use too much power too
quickly pay a deadly price.
