                                        Where's My Maneuver?                                                                                                Elementalist

                     Since most other classes get a bespoke maneuver, you
                     might find yourself asking, "Where's the special maneuver
                     for the fury?" The answer is that the class doesn't need its
                     own maneuver, because most of the time, the fantasy of the
                     fury has them using the Grab or Knockback maneuvers in
                     combat. They're really good at those maneuvers too, so it
                     doesn't make sense to give you another option that you'll
                     rarely or never use.
