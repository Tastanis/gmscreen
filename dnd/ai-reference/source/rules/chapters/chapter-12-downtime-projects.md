                                                                                                these events can help a Director inject more drama into the processes
                                                                                                of research and crafting.

Unarmed Strike Treasures

A hero who has a kit that uses unarmed strikes, such as the Martial
Artist and Pugilist kit, can create leveled weapon treasures that
enhance their unarmed strikes by imbuing hand wraps, rings, shoes,
or any other item worn on the body with supernatural power. At
the Director's discretion, heroes who don't use kits, such as elemen-
talists and nulls, can also benefit from such treasures.

with those people to undertake this project or make project rolls for
it. When you complete this project, your Stamina maximum increases
by 12 + your level until the end of your next respite, and you can't start
another Spend Time With Loved Ones project for a month.
